## Description
Hans Baudler er sergent i [[02 Player/Erukana (Nissen)/Factions/Skjoldungerne\|Skjoldungerne]] (Shield-bearers), en militær enhed i [[02 Player/Erukana (Nissen)/Locations/Astley\|Astley]].
